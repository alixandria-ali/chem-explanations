# Grades 9-10 Chemistry

Welcome to foundational chemistry topics for grades 9-10. These pages cover the essential concepts you'll need to build a strong chemistry foundation.

## Topics Covered

- **[Atomic Structure](atomic-structure.md)**: Understanding atoms, subatomic particles, and electron configuration
- **[Periodic Table](periodic-table.md)**: Organization of elements, trends, and periodic properties
- **[Chemical Bonding](chemical-bonding.md)**: Ionic, covalent, and metallic bonds
- **[Stoichiometry](stoichiometry.md)**: Mole calculations, balancing equations, and limiting reactants
- **[States of Matter](states-of-matter.md)**: Solids, liquids, gases, and phase changes

Each topic includes detailed explanations, worked examples, and practice problems to help you master the material.
