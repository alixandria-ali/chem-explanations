# Grades 11-12 Chemistry

Welcome to advanced chemistry topics for grades 11-12. These pages build on foundational concepts and explore more complex chemical principles.

## Topics Covered

- **[Thermochemistry](thermochemistry.md)**: Energy changes in chemical reactions, enthalpy, and calorimetry
- **[Chemical Equilibrium](equilibrium.md)**: Reversible reactions, equilibrium constants, and Le Chatelier's Principle
- **[Acids and Bases](acids-bases.md)**: pH, acid-base theories, buffers, and titrations
- **[Electrochemistry](electrochemistry.md)**: Redox reactions, galvanic cells, and electrolysis
- **[Chemical Kinetics](kinetics.md)**: Reaction rates, rate laws, and reaction mechanisms

Each topic includes detailed explanations, worked examples, and practice problems to prepare you for advanced chemistry studies.
