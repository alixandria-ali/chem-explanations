# AP Chemistry

Welcome to AP Chemistry content designed for Advanced Placement exam preparation. These pages cover college-level chemistry topics with rigorous problem-solving and conceptual depth.

## Topics Covered

- **[Advanced Atomic Structure](atomic-structure-advanced.md)**: Quantum mechanics, orbitals, and electronic configuration
- **[Thermodynamics](thermodynamics.md)**: Entropy, Gibbs free energy, and spontaneity
- **[Advanced Equilibrium](equilibrium-advanced.md)**: Solubility, complex ions, and advanced calculations
- **[Advanced Kinetics](kinetics-advanced.md)**: Integrated rate laws, reaction mechanisms, and catalysis
- **[Advanced Electrochemistry](electrochemistry-advanced.md)**: Nernst equation, cell potentials, and electrolysis calculations

Each topic provides comprehensive explanations, challenging worked examples, and practice problems aligned with AP Chemistry curriculum and exam expectations.

## AP Exam Tips

- Show all work and units in calculations
- Explain reasoning in free-response questions
- Master significant figures and scientific notation
- Practice with timed problems
- Review laboratory procedures and data analysis
