    Atomic Structure¶

    Key Concepts: - Atomic Number (Z): Number of protons (defines the element) - Mass Number (A): Protons + Neutrons - Isotopes: Same protons, different neutrons - Ions: Atoms that gain or lose electrons - Cations: Positive (lose electrons) - Anions: Negative (gain electrons)

    Important Formulas: - Neutrons = Mass Number - Atomic Number - \( \text{Neutrons} = A - Z \)

    Atomic Notation: - \( ^A_Z X \) where X is the element symbol

    Electron Configuration Rules: 1. Aufbau Principle: Fill lowest energy orbitals first 2. Pauli Exclusion Principle: Max 2 electrons per orbital 3. Hund's Rule: Fill orbitals singly before pairing
